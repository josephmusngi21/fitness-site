Calfs

Calf Raises

This exercise aims to stimulate the calf region of your legs. Performing this exercise the correct way will result in muscle growth in the calves. This exercise can be performed using dumbbells, or machine variations.


How to perform:


  1. Start by standing straight with legs in full extension.
  2. Find an elevated surface standing on the balms of your feet and heels hanging.
  3. Raise your heels as high as possible, contracting your calves.
  4. Lower your heels and reach full stretch of your calves and perform the next repetition.
