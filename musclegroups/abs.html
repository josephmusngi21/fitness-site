Abs

Sit-ups

This exercise aims to stimulate the rectus abdominus. Performing this exercise correctly will strengthen your core. This exercise can be performed with weight or bodyweight.


How to perform:


  1. Start by laying on the floor with your legs bent.
  2. Looking straight up brace your core and lift your torso up to your knees.
  3. Slowly lower your torso back to a laying position and perform the next repetition.

Leg Raises

This exercise aims to stimulate the rectus abdominus. Performing this exercise correctly will strengthen your core. This exercise can be performed with weight or bodyweight.


How to perform:


  1. Start by laying flat on the floor.
  2. Move your hands underneath your glutes or out to your sides.
  3. With your legs fully extended, raise them to a 90 degree angle with the floor.
  4. Slowly lower your legs down just above the floor keeping a contraction in your abs.
  5. Perform the next repetition.
