Biceps

Standing Dumbbell Bicep Curl

This exercise aims to stimulate the biceps found on either arm's upper section. Performing this exercise with the proper form and intensity will result in efficient and effective muscle growth in your biceps. In contrast, other muscles will be recruited at a smaller scale like your forearms but the main focus is the bicep. This exercise can be performed with dumbbells, barbell, or cables.


How to perform:


  1. Start by standing upright with your choice of dumbbells.
  2. Extend arms fully in a supinated position.
  3. While keeping your elbows in place, slowly curl the weight towards your chest area.
  4. Finally slowly lower the weight back down to start the next repetition.
