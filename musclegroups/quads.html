Quadriceps

Seated Leg Extension

This exercise aims to stimulate the quadriceps, glutes, and hamstring muscles of your legs. Performing this exercise will result in effective muscle growth. This exercise is performed on a machine.


How to perform:


  1. Start by sitting on the machine with preferred adjustments.
  2. Hold the side bars and brace your core.
  3. Push your legs forward until your leg is fully extended for full contraction.
  4. Slowly lower the weight and begin the next repetition.
