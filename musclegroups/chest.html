Chest

Flat Barbell Bench Press

This exercise aims to stimulate the pectoralis major with little stimulation on triceps. Performing this exercise will effectively grow muscle in the pectoralis major area, with emphasis on the mid sections of the pecs. This exercise can be performed using dumbbells or a barbell.


How to perform:


  1. Lay flat with your eyes in line directly under the barbell.
  2. Create a slight arch in your lower back and pinch your shoulder blades together.
  3. Have both feet flat on the floor, roughly under your hips.
  4. Press the bar and keep both arms extended.
  5. Finally, lower the weight slowly towards your chest, then press into full extension and start the next rep.
