Tricep

Cable Pushdowns

This exercise aims to stimulate the triceps. Performing this exercise will result in effective growth in all heads of the triceps.


How to perform:


  1. Choose a cable attachment that suits you; v-bar, straight bar, ez bar, rope, or no attachment.
  2. Stand straight in front of the cable and grip your attachment in a pronated position.
  3. With your elbows in full flexion extend by pushing the weight straight down.
  4. Return the weight to reach fuil flexion again and begin the next repetition.
