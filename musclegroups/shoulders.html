Seated Shoulder Press

This exercise aims to stimulate the front head of your deltoids. Performing this exercise will result in effective growth in your front deltoid. This exercise can be performed using dumbbells, barbell, or cables.


How to perform:


  1. Start by sitting straight on a bench or a seat.
  2. Keep your feet planted flat on the floor.
  3. Take your dumbbells or barbbell and hold them above your shoulders next to your ears.
  4. Press over your shoulders into nearly full extension of your elbows.
  5. Finally, lower the weight back down above your shoulders to start the next repetition.
