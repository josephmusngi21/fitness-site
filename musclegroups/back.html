Bent Over Row

This exercise aims to stimulate the several muscles located on your back. The main muscles being the trapezius, lattisimus dorsi, and erector spinae. Performing this exercise with the proper form and intensity will result in efficient and effect muscle growth in those 3 main back muscles and other minor muscles. This can be performed using dumbbells or a barbell.


How to perform:


  1. Start by standing upright with your choice of dumbbell or barbell.
  2. Bend forward with your hips in place and with a straight back.
  3. With your arms in full extension hanging in front of you, pull the weight towards your stomach.
  4. Slowly lower the weight back down to start the next repetition.
